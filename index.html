Types of Coffee Drinks
Espresso:
A strong concentrated coffee drink typically served in a small espresso cup. It is commonly used as a base for many coffee drinks such as lattes, cappuccinos, and macchiatos.
Americano:
A coffee drink typically made from equal parts espresso and hot water and is enjoyed black or with a small amount of cream. Americanos typically have strong flavor with less bitterness.
Iced Coffee:
Iced coffee is made by pouring regular coffee over ice. Iced coffee can be customized to include milk, cream, or flavored syrups. This cold coffee drink is a refreshing alternative to hot coffee during warmer weather.
Flat White:
A coffee drink made by pouring steamed milk over an espresso. This coffee beverage has a higher amount of espresso than milk and is known for its rich, bold flavor, and velvety texture.
Mocha:
A coffee drink known for its chocolatey sweet, rich, and indulgent flavor. Mocha is made with steamed milk, chocolate syrup, and espresso with a layer of foam added to the top. This coffee beverage can be enjoyed hot or cold.
Long Macchiato:
Long macchiato is similar to how a traditional macchiato is prepared but with a double espresso instead of a single shot. This coffee drink is made with double espresso and a small amount of steamed milk on top. A long macchiato is typically a stronger coffee with a small amount of creamy texture.
